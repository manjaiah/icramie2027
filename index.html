About the Conference

The International Conference on Recent Advances in Manufacturing Innovation and Engineering aims to provide a premier platform to showcase cutting-edge developments in advanced manufacturing and industrial engineering.

Conference Focus

The conference covers additive manufacturing and welding, advanced materials, material design and characterization, smart manufacturing, Industry 4.0/5.0, AI and machine learning for intelligent data-driven production systems, operations research, supply chain management, quality engineering, industrial design, advanced machining and forming, and computational modelling and simulation.

Who Should Attend?

Experts, scholars, researchers, faculty members, students, industry professionals, scientific officers and technical officers working in advanced manufacturing, industrial engineering, Industry 4.0/5.0, AI/ML and related applications are warmly invited.

Key Themes

A multidisciplinary forum for emerging technologies, collaborations and innovation in manufacturing and industrial engineering.

About NIT Warangal

National Institute of Technology Warangal, formerly Regional Engineering College, was established in 1959. It is a premier technical institute with 13 departments, undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programmes, a fully residential 250-acre campus, and strong infrastructure including laboratories, library, seminar halls and guest houses.
