About the Conference

The International Conference on Recent Advances in Manufacturing Innovation and Engineering aims to provide a premier platform for researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policymakers to exchange knowledge and showcase cutting-edge developments in advanced manufacturing and industrial engineering.

Conference Focus

The conference covers additive manufacturing and welding, advanced materials, material design and characterization, smart manufacturing, Industry 4.0/5.0, AI & machine learning for intelligent data-driven production systems, industrial engineering, operations research, supply chain management, quality engineering, product design, advanced machining and forming, and computational modelling and simulation.

Who Should Attend?

Experts, scholars, researchers, faculty members, students, industry professionals, scientific officers, and technical officers working in advanced manufacturing, industrial engineering, Industry 4.0/5.0, AI & ML and related applications are warmly invited.

About NIT Warangal

National Institute of Technology Warangal, formerly Regional Engineering College, was established in 1959. It is a premier technical institute with 13 departments, undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programmes, a fully residential 250-acre campus, and strong infrastructure including laboratories, library, seminar halls and guest houses.

Honouring Prof. Krishnanand Lanka

Lifetime Contribution Tribute

Prof. Krishnanand Lanka

Professor (HAG), Department of Mechanical Engineering
National Institute of Technology, Warangal

The organizing committee of the conference proudly honors Prof. Krishnanand Lanka on the momentous occasion of his upcoming superannuation on 31st March 2027.

With nearly 41 years of exemplary service at NIT Warangal, Prof. Lanka has been a pillar of academic excellence, institutional leadership, and groundbreaking research in Industrial Engineering, CAD/CAM, and Additive Manufacturing. Beyond his administrative roles—including heading the School of Management and the Siemens Centre of Excellence—his true legacy lies in the thousands of engineering careers and research minds he has shaped.

We invite colleagues, alumni, and attendees to join us at the conference in expressing our deepest gratitude to Prof. Lanka for his lifetime of dedication to the engineering community and wishing him a fulfilling next chapter.
